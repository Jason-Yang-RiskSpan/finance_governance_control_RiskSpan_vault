🏛️ Regulatory Compliance Framework

Aligned with global banking standards

  • SR 11-7 (Fed/OCC): Model Risk Management "gold standard"
    • Three pillars: Development, Independent Validation, Governance
  • CFPB - AI Explainability: Adverse action transparency
    • "Too complex to explain" is NOT a valid defense
  • Third-Party AI Risk: Vendor due diligence & audit rights

✅ AI Model Validation Checklist

Four-phase validation based on NIST AI RMF & SR 11-7

  1. Conceptual Soundness: Clear objectives, appropriate logic, documented data
  2. Performance Testing: Out-of-sample accuracy, stress testing, failure modes
  3. Fairness & Anti-Bias: Disparate impact analysis, proxy variable checks
  4. Operational Controls: HITL processes, drift alerts, board approval
