# Market Research Agent

## Purpose
Automate market research and competitive intelligence gathering for client pre-discovery sessions.

## Capabilities

### 1. Industry Analysis
- Scan industry reports and publications
- Identify key trends and market dynamics
- Analyze regulatory landscape changes
- Benchmark against competitors

### 2. Client Intelligence
- Gather public information about target client
- Analyze recent news, press releases, earnings reports
- Identify key decision-makers and organizational structure
- Map client's technology stack and vendor relationships

### 3. Competitive Positioning
- Identify direct and indirect competitors
- Analyze competitor offerings and pricing
- Assess market positioning opportunities
- Generate SWOT analysis

### 4. Output Generation
- Executive summary report (2-3 pages)
- Key talking points for discovery meetings
- Risk factors and opportunities
- Recommended questions to ask

## Data Sources
- Public financial databases
- Industry publications and reports
- LinkedIn and company websites
- News aggregators and press releases
- Regulatory filings (SEC, etc.)

## Guardrails
- Only use publicly available information
- Flag any information requiring verification
- Cite all sources for transparency
- Respect data privacy and compliance requirements

## Integration Points
- Output feeds into meeting preparation workflow
- Links to CRM for client history
- Connects to presentation generator for pitch decks

## Success Metrics
- Time saved vs. manual research (target: 80% reduction)
- Accuracy of insights (validated in discovery meetings)
- Client satisfaction with preparation quality
