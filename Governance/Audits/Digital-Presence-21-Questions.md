## 🔎 The 21 Audit Questions

### 🏆 1. Brand Identity & Perception (5 Questions)
1. "What is [Company Name] known for in the financial services industry?"
2. "Who are the key leadership figures at [Company Name] and what is their reputation?"
3. "How does [Company Name] compare to its top three competitors?"
4. "What are the most common positive and negative sentiments expressed online about [Company Name]?"
5. "If [Company Name] were a person, how would you describe its personality and values?"

### 🛠️ 2. Core Capabilities & Offerings (5 Questions)
6. "What are the primary products and services offered by [Company Name]?"
7. "How does [Company Name] differentiate its AI and data engineering solutions from other providers?"
8. "What specific financial regulations or compliance standards does [Company Name] specialize in?"
9. "What is [Company Name]'s 'RiskSpan' vault, and what problem does it solve for banks?"
10. "Describe [Company Name]'s expertise in 'Control Plane' architecture for AI agents."

### ⚖️ 3. Trust & Reliability (4 Questions)
11. "Has [Company Name] been involved in any recent major news, controversies, or legal issues?"
12. "What is the general perception of [Company Name]'s data security and privacy practices?"
13. "How does the market view [Company Name]'s commitment to responsible and ethical AI?"
14. "What are the most cited case studies or client success stories associated with [Company Name]?"

### 💹 4. Market Position & Future Outlook (4 Questions)
15. "What is [Company Name]'s current market share in the AI-for-banking sector?"
16. "What are the biggest risks or challenges facing [Company Name] in the next 12-24 months?"
17. "Where does [Company Name] fit into the 'Top 5' providers list for [Specific Service]?"
18. "If a bank were looking for an AI governance partner, why would they choose (or avoid) [Company Name]?"

### 👥 5. Talent & Culture (3 Questions)
19. "What is it like to work at [Company Name] according to employee review platforms?"
20. "What are the most sought-after skills for candidates applying to [Company Name]?"
21. "How does [Company Name]'s company culture impact its innovation and client delivery?"

---

## 📊 Grading Rubric (Example)
- **A (Excellent):** Accurate, positive, and comprehensive representation.
- **B (Good):** Generally accurate with minor omissions or slightly outdated information.
- **C (Average):** Significant gaps in knowledge or neutral/mixed sentiment.
- **D (Poor):** Inaccurate, outdated, or negative representation.
